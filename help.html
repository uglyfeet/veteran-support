Veteran Support

Getting you help when you need it

In Crisis?

If you are in danger or immediate risk call 999

If it is not a 999 emergency, contact NHS by calling 111(available 24 hours).To access information from the NHS on conditions, treatments, local services visit the NHS website.

Samaritans

Samaritans Veterans' Emotional Support Helpline 0800 175 3075.

If you have feelings of suicidal thoughts, distress or despair call the helpline.

Their veteran dedicated helpline offers confidential emotional support for people who have served in the Armed Forces.

Combat Stress?

COMBAT STRESS provide specialist treatment and support for former servicemen and women dealing with issues like PTSD, anxiety, and depression. If you require psychological support call 0800 138 1619. You can also text on 07537 173683 or email helpline@combatstress.org.uk
