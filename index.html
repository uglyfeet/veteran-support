What can we offer?

Feel healthy

We run exercise sessions to help you stay fit and healthy. We run a variety including seated exercises and low impact exercises.

Feel strong

Our exercise regime is designed to help you build strength and improve your overall fitness.

Feel free

we run a program of discussions and support groups to help you feel more connected and supported.

Get social

We organize social events and gatherings to help you connect with other veterans and build a supportive community.
